# User Manual
## Target Audience: Petugas Lapangan & Field Personnel

Welcome to the SIMDATAN system manual. This guide explains how to perform daily operations on the platform.

---

### 1. Getting Started & Logging In
1. Open your browser and navigate to the SIMDATAN URL.
2. Enter your registered email address (e.g., `petugas@simdatan.go.id`) and password.
3. Check the "Remember Me" box to stay logged in.
4. Click **Login**. You will be directed to the main Dashboard.

---

### 2. Navigating the Dashboard
- **KPI Summary Cards**: Review total farmers, registered lands, and current seasonal production figures.
- **Charts**: View commodity summaries and regional crop breakdowns.

---

### 3. Managing Farmers (Petani)
1. In the sidebar, select **Petani**.
2. Click **+ Tambah Petani** in the top right.
3. Complete the form:
   - Input the **16-digit NIK** (must be unique).
   - Enter their Full Name, Date of Birth, Gender, Alamat, and Nomor HP.
   - Select their **Kelompok Tani** from the dropdown menu.
   - Upload their Profile Picture (if available).
4. Click **Simpan**.

---

### 4. Logging Lahan & Coordinates
1. Select **Lahan** from the sidebar.
2. Click **+ Tambah Lahan**.
3. Choose the owner (Petani) from the dropdown list.
4. Enter the Land Name and area in Hectares (e.g. `1.50`).
5. Choose their Ownership Status (Milik Sendiri / Sewa / Bagi Hasil).
6. Enter the **Latitude** and **Longitude** values (or click on the Leaflet map to place a pin).
7. Upload land certificate drafts or photographs.
8. Click **Simpan**.

---

### 5. Reporting Harvest Cycles
1. Select **Produksi Panen** from the sidebar.
2. Click **+ Input Produksi**.
3. Select the Lahan and the Commodity (Padi, Jagung, etc.).
4. Input the Season (e.g., "Musim Hujan 1") and Planting Date.
5. Set initial status as `Sedang Tanam`.
6. Once the harvest completes:
   - Open the record, click Edit.
   - Change Status to `Sudah Panen`.
   - Input the Harvest Date and the Net Yield (in Tons).
   - The system automatically calculates productivity index.
7. Click **Submit for Review**. This forwards the entry to the Supervisor.
